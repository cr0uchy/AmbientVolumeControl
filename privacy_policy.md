# Privacy Policy for Ambient Volume Control

Last updated: February 2026

Ambient Volume Control does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or audio recordings.

The app uses the device microphone solely to measure ambient noise levels in real time. All audio processing occurs locally on the device. No audio data is ever recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

The app has no internet permission and makes no network connections.

No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools are used.

If you have any questions, please contact: cr0uchy@github.com
